Stay Safe Online–Prevention Checklist(for College Students)

1.Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts & avoid reusing passwords.
2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
3.Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments.
4.Verify website URLs before entering personal or login details.
5.Avoid sharing sensitive personal information on public platforms.
6.Regularly update your apps, devices, and antivirus software.
7.Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid accessing sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
8.Log out from shared or public devices after use.
9.Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
10.Always verify payment requests before approving; avoid “collect request” scams.
11.Use only official and trusted payment apps.
--Reporting:
Report cyber fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud.
