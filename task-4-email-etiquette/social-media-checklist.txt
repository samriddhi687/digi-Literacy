Here is a quick checklist of Do’s and Don’ts to help you manage your digital footprint while in college:
The "Do" List-
1.Maintain a respectful and professional tone in posts and comments.
2.Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
3.Adjust privacy settings to control who can view your content.
4.Share content that reflects your skills, achievements and interests.
5.Think before posting—consider your digital footprint.
6.Give proper credit when using others’ content.
7.Use social media for learning, networking and opportunities.

The "Don't" List-
1.Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2.Avoid posting offensive, abusive or inappropriate content.
3.Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
4.Avoid oversharing details about your daily life or location.
5.Do not spread rumors or unverified news.
6.Avoid using weak passwords or sharing login credentials.
7.Do not let excessive usage affect your studies or productivity.
